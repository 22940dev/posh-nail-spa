About Posh

"Every woman can wear a great outfit, but it is her nails that make the statement."

Tammy Taylor

Posh Nail Spa was founded in 2013 by co-founders Jimmy & Julie Tran with the purpose of bringing chic and vibrant nail designs for the local beauty lovers. Posh Nail Spa is typically known as a professional and friendly environment with experienced technicians who always love to build a long-lasting relationship with their customers.

By addressing our customers' needs, we always keep our technicians update with new designing trends, and try to apply them to customers' nails if they request

Come to our store to enjoy:

  • Posh nail designs with a competitive price.
  • A friendly and professional environment.
  • Experienced and dedicated nail technicians who always value customers' satisfaction.
